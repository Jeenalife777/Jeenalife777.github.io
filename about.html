About Jeena Weber Langstaff:

Jeena Weber Langstaff was born in Northern India and then at the age of two she moved to Switerland. There she lived for 7 years. In 2007 she moved to Rhode Island with her family. Since moving to the United States, Jeena has commmitted 7 years to a classical indian dance form called Bharatanatyam. She finished her dancing in 2017, after a 4.5 hour long performance. Jeena received a bachelor's in Business Management and International Business in 2021 at Goucher College in Baltimore, Maryland. She then took a few years from academia, and worked in the business field, and then of fall of 2023, she volunteered in South Africa for 12 weeks in nature conservation and animal preservation. Then this fall of 2024, she has decided to go back to school at University of Rhode Island for a BA and Masters in Computer Science and another Masters in Psychology.
